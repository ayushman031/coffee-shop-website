Our Story

Born From Passion,
Brewed with Purpose

Founded in 2010 in Bengaluru, Chai & Brew started as a tiny stall serving authentic South Indian coffee to morning commuters. Today, we've grown into a beloved chain with outlets across India, but our soul remains the same.

We source beans directly from estates in Coorg, Wayanad, and Chikmagalur — supporting local farmers and ensuring freshness in every cup.

Single-origin, ethically sourced beans
In-house roasting every morning
Certified SCA baristas
Vegan & dairy-free options available
