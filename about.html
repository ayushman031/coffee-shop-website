A Cup That Changed Everything

It all began with a single cup. In 2010, our founder Rajan Pillai, a coffee planter's son from Coorg, set up a humble stall outside a Bengaluru railway station. His filter coffee — made from beans his family had grown for generations — became an overnight sensation.

Word spread. The stall became a café. The café became a chain. Today, Chai & Brew has 18 outlets across 7 cities in India — from Mumbai to Chennai, Delhi to Kochi. But our soul remains rooted in those Coorg hills.

We are proud to be 100% Indian — sourcing from Indian farms, employing local baristas, and serving beverages that celebrate India's incredible coffee and tea heritage.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Sustainability

We are committed to eco-friendly practices — biodegradable packaging, zero food waste, and supporting sustainable farming communities in Coorg and Wayanad.

Community

5% of every cup sold goes to our farmer welfare fund, directly supporting the estate workers who grow our exceptional coffee beans.

Excellence

Every barista at Chai & Brew is SCA-certified and undergoes 120 hours of training before they serve their first cup to a customer.
